UiPath Culture At a Glance

371 employees at UiPath have reviewed UiPath across various culture dimensions, providing their opinions on items ranging from executive ratings to the pace at work. The latest review was 15 days ago.

Overall, the 371 UiPath employees give their leadership a grade of A+, or Top 5% of similar size companies on Comparably. This includes specific ratings of their executive team, CEO, and manager.

Employees at UiPath are extremely happy with their total compensation at UiPath, which includes a combination of pay, stock and equity, and benefits.

Overall, employees at UiPath are extremely happy with their team. 371 Participants grade the quality of their coworkers an A+. The majority believe the meetings at UiPath are effective, and the majority look forward to interacting with their coworkers.

The majority of employees at UiPath believe the environment at UiPath is positive. Most Participants believe the pace of work at UiPath is extremely fast. About 38% of the employees at UiPath work 8 hours or less, while 4% of them have an extremely long day - longer than twelve hours.

Overall, the employees at UiPath are extremely happy, based on their aggregated ratings of future outlook, customer perception, and their excitement going to work.
